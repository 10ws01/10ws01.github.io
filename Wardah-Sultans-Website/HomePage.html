Appleton Weekly

Your weekly source for all things Appleton!

Appleton Weekly is a student-run organisation that updates you on the happenings of Appleton, weekly! We are a team of exactly one person, who ensure our authenticity and reliability by a variety including our own opinions as well as consulting our imaginations. We aim to provide the public with really, truly, entirely, and wholly honest, genuine, and real reports of local happenings. We hope you enjoy our weekly tidbits, and thanks for reading!
